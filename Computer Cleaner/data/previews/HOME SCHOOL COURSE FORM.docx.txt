SRA HOME SCHOOL COURSE FORM
To have a home school course accepted as credit toward SRA school requirements and included on a SRA transcript, parents should:
Discuss with the Secondary Coordinator for approval 
Complete parts 1-5 of this form
Submit form to the Secondary Administrator before the beginning of the school year.  
At the end of First Semester and Second Semester, parents should submit: 
the number of hours spent on the course 
a grade for that semester 
a brief narrative describing the student’s progress  
This document will be kept in the student’s records on Sycamore.  Home school courses will not be on semester report cards, but will be added onto student transcripts at the end of the school year.
